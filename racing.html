--- layout: page title: Racing Team --- The University of Minnesota Cycling Team is a USA Cycling licensed Division 1 collegiate racing team competing in the [North Central Collegiate Cycling Conference](http://nc-cycling.com), which we have won at the team level for 16 years running!
